## Transportation: SFO → GitHub Headquarters
- **Option 1 – BART & Muni:**
  1. Take BART from SFO Airport Station (Yellow Line) to *Montgomery St* station (~30 min).
  2. Transfer to Muni Metro **T** or **J** line toward *Embarcadero*; get off at *Embarcadero* station (~5 min).
  3. Walk north on *The Embarcadero* and turn left onto *Colin P Kelly Jr St* – the GitHub campus is a short 5‑minute walk.
- **Option 2 – Ride‑share (Uber/Lyft):** Approximately 20‑25 min (≈ $25‑$35) depending on traffic.
- **Option 3 – Airport Shuttle + BART:** Some hotels provide a shuttle to the BART Millbrae station; then follow the BART route above.

*All public‑transport schedules can be checked via the Bay Area Transit (BART) app or Google Maps.*
